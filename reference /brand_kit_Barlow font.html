04

Brand Voice

Tone 01
Raw & Direct
No fluff. No motivational poster clichés. We say what we mean. Short sentences. Real talk.
Tone 02
Earned, Not Given
We don't celebrate where you're going — we celebrate that you showed up. Progress is personal.
Tone 03
Community First
We speak as a collective — "us", "we", "our people". You're not alone in the grind.
Tone 04
Quietly Confident
Never arrogant, never desperate. The work speaks. We don't need to shout about it.

06

Do's & Don'ts

Do
Use Barlow Condensed for all headlines and the logo
Keep burnt orange as an accent — use it sparingly
Maintain generous white space around the logo
Use dark, cinematic photography with film grain
Write copy that is short, direct and earned
Use all-caps for the brand name always
Don't
Stretch or distort the logo in any way
Use bright orange (#ff6a00) — only burnt orange (#d45c00)
Place the logo on busy or cluttered backgrounds
Use stock-style, overly polished gym photography
Use more than three colours in any single layout
Use fonts other than Barlow Condensed and Barlow
